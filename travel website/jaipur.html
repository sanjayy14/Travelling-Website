Jaipur, Rajasthan

"Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is the capital of Rajasthan and a vibrant destination famous for its rich history and magnificent architecture. The city boasts stunning palaces, grand forts, and vibrant bazaars. Visitors can explore the opulent City Palace, the iconic Hawa Mahal, and the formidable Amber Fort, while also experiencing the local culture through traditional crafts and cuisines. Jaipur's colorful streets and warm hospitality make it a must-visit destination in India."
