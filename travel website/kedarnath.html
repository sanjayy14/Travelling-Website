Kedarnath, Uttarakhand

"Kedarnath, a sacred town nestled in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand, is one of the holiest pilgrimage sites in India. Home to the famous Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, the town attracts thousands of devotees every year. Surrounded by majestic snow-capped peaks and serene landscapes, Kedarnath offers a spiritual and tranquil retreat. The journey to Kedarnath is both a physical and spiritual adventure, with breathtaking views and a profound sense of devotion."
