Manali,Himachal Pradesh

"Nestled in the Himalayas of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is a picturesque hill station renowned for its stunning natural beauty and adventurous spirit. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and meandering rivers, Manali offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Visitors can explore ancient temples, go trekking amidst pine forests, indulge in winter sports like skiing and snowboarding, or simply unwind amidst the serene landscapes."
